About MLBB

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Moonton. In the game, two teams of five players each compete to destroy the opposing team's base while defending their own. The current meta emphasizes strategic gameplay and character balance, with frequent updates introducing new heroes and adjustments to gameplay mechanics.
