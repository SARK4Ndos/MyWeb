Introduction

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. The game is free-to-play and is only monetized through in-game purchases like characters and skins. Each player can control a selectable character, called a Hero, with unique abilities and traits.

Responsible Gaming & Community

Mobile Legends brings players together from around the world. Let’s ensure our actions promote a positive, inclusive community where everyone feels welcome.

Support Clean Water Initiatives

While you enjoy your games, consider how you can contribute to clean water efforts worldwide. Every small action counts towards a sustainable future!
